Personal Life

I was born on June 15, 1975, in London, England. I grew up in a football-loving family and developed a passion for the sport from a young age. I played football throughout my childhood and adolescence, and went on to study Sports Science at the University of Manchester.

Off the field, I have always been known for my dedication to fitness and healthy living. I am also an avid runner and enjoy hiking in my free time. I am also a philanthropist and also support several charitable organizations that promote youth sports and education.
